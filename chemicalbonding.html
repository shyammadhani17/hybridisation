TYPES OF HYBRIDISATION

1) sp HYBRIDISATION

  • In sp hybridisation one s- and one p- orbital of an atom are mixed to givetwo new hybrid which are equivalent in shape and energy.
  • Bond angle is of 180o.
  • s-character is 50%.
  • It is planar in shape.

2) sp2 HYBRIDISATION

  • In sp2 hybridisation one s- and two p- orbitals are mixed to give three new hybrid orbitals of same shape and equivalent energies.
  • Angle between orbitals is 120o.
  • s-character is 33.3%.
  • It has trigonal planar shape.

3) sp3 HYBRIDISATION

  • In sp3 hybridisation one s- and three p- orbitals are mixed to give new hybrid orbitals of same shape and equivalent energies.
  • Angle between orbitals is 120o28'.
  • s-character is 33.3%.
  • It has trigonal planar shape.

4) sp3d HYBRIDISATION

  • In sp3d hybridisation one s- and four p- and one d orbitals are mixed to give new hybrid orbitals of same shape and equivalent energies.
  • Angle between three orbitals is 120o and 2 orbitals are perpendicular.
  • It has trigonal bipyramidal shape.

5) sp3d2 HYBRIDISATION

  • In sp3d2 hybridisation one s- and four p- and two d orbitals are mixed to give new hybrid orbitals of same shape and equivalent energies.
  • Angle between all orbitals is 90o
  • It has trigonal symmetrical octahedral shape.
